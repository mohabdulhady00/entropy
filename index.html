00 CRYSTAL · SOLID STATE

A world in perfect order.

Everything begins as structure — rigid, faceted, still. Then you touch the scroll wheel, and thermodynamics takes over.

01 FRACTURE · KINETIC

Velocity becomes violence.

Scroll fast and the form can't hold. It shatters into rigid bodies — each fragment a real Cannon-es simulation, tumbling under gravity, colliding, settling. Flick the wheel; watch it break.

02 MELT · LIQUID STATE

Solids forget how to stay solid.

Heat rises with disorder. Surfaces soften, then run — displaced by flowing noise in a WebGL shader until the object drips like wax and the very letters begin to liquefy.

03 VAPOR · GAS STATE

Then it lets go.

Matter loses cohesion entirely. The mesh disperses into a hundred thousand points; the words themselves detach, drift, and rise off the page like smoke. Nothing here is fixed anymore.

04 THE TURN · GRAVITY = 90°

Down is a choice.

Reality rotates. Gravity swings ninety degrees and the whole world becomes a side-scroller — debris now falls sideways, the horizon is a wall, and you are moving through it.

05 SINGULARITY · REFORM

All disorder collapses to one.

Entropy has a punchline: enough chaos, and everything is pulled back into a single point — and made new.
